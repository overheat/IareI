IareI

AI-native terminal for the next generation of human–AI interaction.

Not GUI. Not CLI. But an AI-native console.

What is IareI

IareI explores a new interface paradigm for continuous human–AI interaction. It begins with AConsole — a terminal-like environment for coding agents, edge devices, and real-world automation.

Name

IareI reflects a simple structure: I → are → I. The first I is the human. The second I is the AI. “are” is the connection between them.

Status

Early stage. Direction first. Rapid iteration expected.
